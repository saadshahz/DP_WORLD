  • OUR JOURNEY

    In the 1970s, as a part of Prime Ministerâ€™s program for economic reforms it was decided to establish a purpose-built specialised port facility for bulk handling of massive imports, and thus Port Qasim came into being. The port encompasses a total area of 12,000 acres and its strategic position places it in close proximity to major shipping routes.
